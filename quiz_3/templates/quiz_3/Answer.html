What is the chance that your opponent’s valuation is any given number between 0 and 100? For example, what is the chance that your opponent’s valuation is 53?

Answer: Since their valuation is equally likely to be any whole number between 0 and 100, and there are 101 such numbers, the chance is 1/101 (roughly 1%).
