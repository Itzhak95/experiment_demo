Summary of the auction rules:

  1. You and your opponent both have a valuation between 0 and 100 (all numbers equally likely).
  2. You bid knowing your valuation but not that of your opponent.
  3. You win the auction if you bid strictly more than your opponent does.
  4. If you win the auction, you receive your valuation minus the number of tokens that you bid.
  5. However, if you lose the auction, you still need to pay your bid.
