Summary of the rules:

  1. You must choose a (whole) number between 11 and 20 inclusive.
  2. You will be paid (in tokens) whatever number you choose.
  3. If the number you choose is exactly one lower than the number chosen by your opponent, you receive an additional bonus of 20 tokens.
  4. If the number you choose is the same as the number chosen by your opponent, you receive a bonus of 10 tokens.
